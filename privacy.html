Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

1. Information We Collect

  • Tracking numbers you enter or import from Gmail
  • Device information for push notifications
  • Usage data to improve the App experience

2. Device Permissions

WhereIsIt requests the following permissions, which are used only on your device:

  • Camera — used only when you choose to scan a shipping label or receipt. Images are processed on your device to read the tracking number and are never uploaded or stored by us.
  • Location — optional. Used only on your device to center the map and show where your packages are relative to you. Your location is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

3. Gmail Access

If you choose to connect your Gmail account, we access your email solely to identify shipping confirmation emails and extract tracking numbers. We request read-only access (gmail.readonly) and do not read, store, or share the content of your emails beyond what is necessary for package detection.

We do not access your Gmail when the app is not actively scanning for packages. You can disconnect your Gmail account at any time from the app's settings.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide package tracking services
  • To send push notifications about delivery updates
  • To sync your data across devices via iCloud (if enabled)
  • To improve the App's features and performance

5. Data Storage

Your package list, names, addresses, delivery history, and email content stay on your device and are optionally synced to your own private iCloud account. We do not store any of that on our servers.

6. Tracking Proxy

To look up shipment status, the app sends the tracking number and carrier name to our secure proxy service (hosted on Cloudflare), which forwards them to the relevant carrier and returns the result to your device. The proxy exists so that carrier API credentials are never embedded in the app.

The proxy uses Apple App Attest to confirm requests come from a genuine copy of the app, and keeps only minimal, anonymous abuse-prevention data: a per-installation identifier (not linked to your name, email, or Apple ID) and request counts. It does not store your package history, names, addresses, or email content.

7. Third-Party Services

We use third-party carrier APIs (UPS, DHL, and the Ship24 aggregator) to retrieve tracking information. These services receive only the tracking number to look up shipment status. Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle that data.

8. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Tracking numbers are sent only to carrier APIs for the purpose of retrieving delivery status.

9. Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App. iCloud data follows Apple's retention policies. You can delete individual packages or all data from within the app.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Google API Services User Data Policy

WhereIsIt's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. We only use Gmail data to identify shipping confirmation emails and extract tracking numbers. We do not use Gmail data for advertising, market research, or any purpose unrelated to package tracking.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the App and on this page.

13. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@whereisit.app.
